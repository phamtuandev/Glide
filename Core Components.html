  • TextureAnimatorComponent

    Component that is used to play texture animation actions on the entity’s sprite node. Uses SKActions for the actual animations and uses predefined trigger ids to switch between playing animations.

    Required components: SpriteNodeComponent

    Component that is used to play audio clips on the entity’s sprite node. Uses SKAudioNodess as clips for playing audio and uses predefined trigger ids to play/stop associated clips.
